但是这种方法

  1. 不能直接在vscode的Markdown插件中预览

Data Preparation:

Prepare your review data, ensuring it is labeled with one of the three classes (-1, 0, 1).
Split your data into a training set and a test set.

Text Preprocessing:

Tokenize your text data (split it into individual words or tokens).
Optionally, remove stopwords (common words that may not carry much meaning) and perform stemming or lemmatization to reduce words to their base form.

Feature Extraction:

Convert your text data into a numerical representation that can be used by machine learning algorithms. One common approach is using TF-IDF (Term Frequency-Inverse Document Frequency) to convert text into numerical features.

Model Training:

交大医学院课表信息提取

Wakeup课程表你匹配了个锤子！
哦可能不是课程表的问题，反正Wakeup课程表和学校教务网站总得有一个背锅（点头

问题

Wakeup课程表无法通过学校教务导入本学期课表（但是能够导入上学期的课表）

解决流程

  1. 可以看到通过学校教务导入大概长这样
  2. 在电脑端复制网页的HTML文件直接通过HTML导入，显示失败
  3. 阅读网页源代码，看到以下代码
  4. 将https://webvpn2.shsmu.edu.cn/https/<some_code>/Home/Timetable改为https://webvpn2.shsmu.edu.cn/https/<some_code>/Home/GetCurriculumTable，返回值为{"Title":null,"List":null,"List2":null,"StuExam":null,"Times":null}
  5. 拷打ChatGPT，在Inspect的Network选项卡中找到https://webvpn2.shsmu.edu.cn/https/<some_code>/Home/GetCurriculumTable?vpn-12-o2-jwstu.shsmu.edu.cn&Start=2024-02-17&End=2024-02-18
  6. 虽然不知道是个什么玩意，但是Start和End看着明显不对，使用https://webvpn2.shsmu.edu.cn/https/<some_code>/Home/GetCurriculumTable?vpn-12-o2-jwstu.shsmu.edu.cn&Start=2024-02-17&End=2024-08-21得到课表数据，格式与(1)中一致。
  7. 保存后通过html导入，成功
